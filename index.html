About Us

The National Service Scheme (NSS) is a Central Sector Scheme of Government of India, Ministry of Youth Affairs & Sports. The KITS Warangal unit of NSS consists of 400 Volunteers, along with a core team guided by the Program Officers. NSS Volunteers take part in various government led community service activities & programmes.

The sole aim of the NSS is to provide hands on experience to young students in delivering community service. The NSS commitee consists of Dr. CH. Sathish Chandra as Programme Officer. The student body is lead by S. Sairam Reddy as General Secretary.

Our Events

  • Major Event

    Blood Donation

    Every year, National Service Scheme, KITS Warangal organises Blood donation camp. On 7th February, 2024 a blood donation camp was organised by NSS in association with the Indian Red Cross Society(IRCS), Warangal. The camp was a huge success with more than 533 people donating blood. Students and Staffs participated in large numbers and donated blood with the help of IRCS and NSS volunteers. More than 1500 lives can be saved with this event.

  • Major Event

    Traffic Awareness

    National Service Scheme, KITS Warangal organises Traffic Awareness in assosiation with the Traffic Police and District Youth office for the well being & safety of the society. We understand the importance of safety. This allows the people in campus to contribute their bit to the society by saving someone's life.

  • Major Event

    Tree Plantation

    National Service Scheme, KITS Warangal organises tree plantation drive regularly in the campus with a motto of green campus. We have planted more than thousands of plants around the campus. We not only plant saplings but also ensure that they get proper nourishment.

    Swachchta Drive

    To make people aware of the importance of cleanliness in their life, NSS KITS Warangal monthly organises Swachchta Drives in which NSS volunteers march around the campus chanting slogans for Swachchta and cleaning the surroundings they pass in the campus.
